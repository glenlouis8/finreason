I fine-tuned a 7B LLM to do math over SEC filings. No labeled data, no team, just me and a Colab notebook.

The base model answered 0.3% of FinQA questions correctly. After fine-tuning: 56.5%.

FinQA is brutal — questions like "what was the 3-year CAGR of operating income" where you need to read a table, do multi-step arithmetic, and get the exact number. The base model just hallucinates. The fine-tuned one actually reasons through it step by step.

Built the full pipeline myself:
- QLoRA SFT on Qwen2.5-7B (4-bit, runs on a single A100)
- DPO alignment using automatically mined preference pairs
- W&B experiment tracking
- Published to HuggingFace, SageMaker deployment scripts ready

Model → https://huggingface.co/glen-louis/finreason-qwen2.5-7b-dpo
Code → https://github.com/glenlouis8/finreason
